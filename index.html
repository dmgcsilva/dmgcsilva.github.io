Short Bio

My name is Diogo Silva and I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the Nova School of Science and Technology, Portugal. My Ph.D. is supported by a CMU Portugal Scholarship under the Affiliated Ph.D. Program.

My reasearch focus is Natural Language Generation(NLG) for Task-Orieneted Dialog Agents. Specifically I focus on making the answers more human like by introducing and varying human traits such as tone of voice and emotions.
